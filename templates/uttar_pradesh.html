Uttar Pradesh - The Land of Diversity

Explore the historical monuments, spiritual sites, and rich cultural heritage of Uttar Pradesh.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, an iconic symbol of love, is a stunning white marble mausoleum located in Agra. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

The Lion Capital of Ashoka

The Lion Capital of Ashoka is an iconic sculpture from the 3rd century BCE, originally erected atop a pillar by Emperor Ashoka in Sarnath, Uttar Pradesh, India. The capital is renowned for its four majestic lions, positioned back-to-back, symbolizing power and authority...

Fatehpur Sikri

Fatehpur Sikri, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, was the capital of the Mughal Empire during the reign of Akbar. The city is renowned for its well-preserved palaces, mosques, and courtyards showcasing Mughal architecture.
